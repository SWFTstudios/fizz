# Steel Navy

**Color theory:** Cool monochromatic — steel blue accent on deep navy ink, ice paper text.

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Accent | `#6b9fd4` |
| Ink | `#0f1a2e` |
| Paper | `#e8edf5` |

**Section intent:** Calm, hydration-tech feel. Statement band uses cool paper `#e8edf5` on navy ink text.

**Contrast:** Strong paper-on-navy for body; accent readable on hero at large sizes.
