# NextFil Email Templates — Fizz Charge

Customize in **NextFil Subscriptions → Customer Email Templates**.

## Renewal confirmation

**Subject:** Your Fizz Charge 6-pack refill is on the way

**Body highlights:**

- You were charged **$10.00** for one **6-pack** of Fizz Charge CO₂ canisters
- Each canister carbonates up to 12 liters
- Manage or request your next refill at `/account?view=subscriptions`

## Payment failure

**Subject:** Action needed — Fizz Charge refill payment

**Body highlights:**

- We couldn't process your $10.00 refill payment
- Update your payment method in your account to keep refills flowing
- Link: `/account?view=subscriptions`

## Subscription welcome (first order)

**Subject:** You're subscribed to Fizz Charge refills

**Body highlights:**

- Welcome to on-demand CO₂ refills — reorder a 6-pack for **$10** whenever you run low
- No fixed schedule; request a refill from your account when you need it
- Cancel anytime from `/account?view=subscriptions`
