# Lime Fizz

**Color theory:** High-contrast brand default — electric lime accent on near-black ink with warm paper text.

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Accent | `#c6f24e` |
| Ink | `#080a0d` |
| Paper | `#f2efe7` |

**Section intent:** Hero and footer on ink; statement band inverted to paper. Step strokes and CTAs use lime accent.

**Contrast:** Accent on ink passes for display type; verify small eyebrow text.
